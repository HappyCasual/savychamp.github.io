Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

SavyChamp ("we", "us", or "our") provides mobile applications including, but not limited to, Jig Cards: Multi Art Puzzle, Tamcall, Arrows Paint, Panda Sort, Love Merge, and Emoji Sentence Puzzle. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our apps and services.

1. Information We Collect

As an independent developer, we respect your privacy and aim to collect only what is necessary to provide a great gaming experience. We may collect:

  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device information, such as your device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to optimize app performance, especially for low-end devices.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data regarding how you interact with our applications (e.g., levels completed, session duration) to improve game design and squash bugs.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Our applications use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Specifically, we utilize Google AdMob to display advertisements within our free applications.

  • Ad networks may use device identifiers and personalized advertising data to serve relevant ads.
  • You can learn more about how Google uses your data by visiting the Google Privacy & Terms site.

3. Children's Privacy

Our applications are designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: contact@savychamp.com.
