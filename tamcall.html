Tamcall - Tambola & Housie Caller

While SavyChamp is primarily known for engaging puzzle games, we also develop practical utility applications designed to bring people together. Tamcall is our digital solution for hosting seamless, organized games of Tambola (also known as Housie or Bingo) for family gatherings, community events, and large groups.

Digitizing a Classic Game

Traditionally, hosting a game of Tambola requires physical boards, numbered tokens, and a designated caller who must manually track drawn numbers. Tamcall eliminates the need for these physical components, which are easily lost or damaged. The application acts as a smart, automated number caller, utilizing a true random number generator (RNG) algorithm to ensure completely fair and unbiased draws every single round.

Focus on Readability and Usability

Because Tambola is a game enjoyed by players of all ages, the user interface of Tamcall was designed with strict accessibility standards. The application features a high-contrast display, ensuring that the current number being called is visible even from a distance. The comprehensive digital board keeps an accurate, instantly readable history of all previously drawn numbers, preventing disputes and keeping the game moving smoothly.

Reliable Performance

Whether you are using a brand-new tablet or an older smartphone, Tamcall is optimized to be incredibly lightweight. It requires minimal system resources, meaning it will run flawlessly on devices with as little as 4GB of RAM without draining the battery during long game nights.
